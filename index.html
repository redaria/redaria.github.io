Hi there, I'm currently a student at the University of Toronto studying towards a specialist degree in computer science, and a minor in economics. I graduated from Don Mills Collegiate Institute and its 6-year CyberARTS program. Over these past 6 years I've created a whole lot of things regarding art and design, and of those whole lot, here shown are some of (what I think are) my best works. They range from digital design to digital painting to print to traditional art and beyond!
Of the themes and ideas I often take inspiration from, games and food are a huge part of that. To be able to work in the gaming industry is my long term goal. And although tea isn't very much of an influence on my work, I feel it is important enough in my life to be mentioned here as well
